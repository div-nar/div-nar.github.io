Now

Updated May 2026

I drudge along at Dognosis, trying to add smidgens of value here and there. As the summers give way to the first rains of the monsoon, I find myself called to return to my work on empathetic machines — to offer the flowers it truly deserves.

The winds grow stronger, but along with them, they bring the weather we Bangaloreans have dearly missed. I force myself now to engage with the classics in an attempt to freshen my mind; so brain-rotting — à la Twitter, Substack and YouTube — cannot get a better hold of me.

I but only wish we all could make time for ourselves, to bestow the privilege of reading on our mortal minds. I'm beginning to prepare myself for a few seasons abroad — but to put in the effort is akin to moving a rock up a mountain.
